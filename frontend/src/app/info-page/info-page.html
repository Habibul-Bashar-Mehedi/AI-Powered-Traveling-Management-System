Help Center

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I book a hotel, tour, or transport?

Sign in and open your dashboard. Browse services published by vendors under "Available Services," choose a quantity and payment method, and confirm your booking.

How do I track a booking?

Open "My Bookings" from your dashboard to see every request's status — pending, confirmed, completed, rejected, or cancelled — updated in real time.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Pending and confirmed bookings can be cancelled directly from "My Bookings," with an optional reason for the vendor.

How do I list my hotel, tour, or transport business?

Create a free account, then register as a vendor from your dashboard. An administrator reviews every application before it goes live, and once approved you can publish services with photos, pricing, and availability.

What can the AI travel assistant help with?

Ask it for destination ideas, itinerary suggestions, or rough budget estimates — it's available directly from your dashboard, any time.

Still need help?

Reach us at support@aiptms.com and we'll get back to you.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and country. When you make a booking, we collect the details needed to fulfill it (dates, quantity, special requests). Vendors provide business details when they register to list services.

How we use your information

We use this information to operate your account, process bookings between you and vendors, provide customer support, and improve the platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing with vendors

When you make a booking, we share the details necessary to fulfil it — such as your name and booking specifics — with the vendor providing that service.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to keep you signed in between visits (authentication tokens). We don't use third-party advertising trackers.

Data security

Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing, and access to administrative functions is role-restricted. No online system is completely risk-free, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Your rights

You can review and update your account details at any time, and you may request account deletion by contacting support.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email support@aiptms.com.
